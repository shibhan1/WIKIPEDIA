***Pawan Kalyan Konidela Kalyan Babu born on 2 September,1971 and he is an Indian politician, actor, filmmaker, and philanthropist.

***He works in Telugu cinema and is the recipient of a Filmfare Award, a SIIMA Award, a CineMAA Award and a Santosham Film Award.

***He has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list in the years 2013, 2017, 2018 .He is also the founder of JanaSenaParty.

***Kalyan founded a political party named Jana Sena Party on 14 March 2014,He wrote a book titled Ism which is also the ideology of the Jana Sena party.
